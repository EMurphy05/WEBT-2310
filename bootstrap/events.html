Sunday Market

The Chattanooga Sunday Market is a weekly community event showcasing local farmers, artists, and food vendors at the First Horizon Pavilion. It’s a lively place to enjoy fresh produce, handmade goods, live music, and a friendly, local atmosphere.

Sport Events

Chattanooga is home to many sports and athletic events. Enjoy the minor-league baseball team, Chattanooga Lookouts or see the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga Mocs play at Finley Stadium. The Chattanooga Football Club is cheered on by soccer fanatics, while the city hosts regular major sporting events like IRONMAN, Head of the Hooch Rowing Regatta, National Athletic Championships, Chattanooga Marathon, and much more.

Concerts & Music

Live Music, festivals, venues, restaurants, clubs, hotels, bars, and even the airport…In Chattanooga, live music is everywhere, every night of the week. Ranging from blues to jazz, bluegrass, pop, rock, and more, Chattanooga live music embraces the diverse sounds that the Scenic City sings.

Annual Festivals

Annual festivals include the Nightfall Friday-night music series, Riverfront Nights, Three Sisters Bluegrass Festival, Chattanooga Hip-Hop Week, Asian Lantern Festival, Jfest, Chattanooga Motorcar Festival, and weekly music and entertainment at the Chattanooga Market – just to name a few.
