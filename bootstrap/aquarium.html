River Journey

The River Journey building explores freshwater ecosystems, starting in the Appalachian Mountains and following waterways around the world. Visitors encounter river otters, turtles, fish, and other wildlife while learning how rivers shape the environment and support diverse life.

Ocean Journey

The Ocean Journey building focuses on saltwater habitats, from coral reefs to open seas. Guests can see sharks, jellyfish, penguins, and colorful reef fish while discovering how ocean environments connect to global ecosystems and daily life.

IMAX

The IMAX Theater near the Tennessee Aquarium offers an immersive movie experience with a giant screen and powerful sound, showcasing educational films about nature, science, and exploration. It’s a great place to dive deeper into the themes of the aquarium and see stunning visuals up close.

Deeper Dives

The Tennessee Aquarium’s Deeper Dives tours offer a behind-the-scenes look at how the aquarium cares for its animals and habitats. Guided by expert staff, visitors can explore areas not normally open to the public, learn about conservation work, and get a closer understanding of what it takes to support aquatic life every day.
